How the ELO System Works
Starting ELO
Every new member starts at 1200 and must complete 5 classes before appearing on the public leaderboard.
K-Factor
New members (<15 classes) use K=40 for faster rating movement. Veterans (100+ classes) use K=10 for stability.
Composite Score
Reps (35%), Rounds (30%), Weight (20%), and Calories (15%) are converted to percentiles within each class session.
Inactivity Decay
After 4 weeks inactive, ELO decays by 5 pts/week down to a floor of 800. Stay active to protect your rank!
