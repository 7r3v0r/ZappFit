04 — FAQ

QUESTIONS?
WE HAVE ANSWERS

What's included in a membership? +
Your membership gives you access to all HIIT and strength classes, full use of the facility, locker rooms, showers, and our community. Unlimited members also get priority booking for high-demand classes.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No cancellation fees, no lock-in periods. Cancel your unlimited membership anytime via our app. Your access ends at the end of your billing cycle. Class packs don't expire until 120 days have passed.
Do I need to book classes in advance? +
No. Drop-ins are welcome anytime. Unlimited members get priority booking if a class is at capacity, but you can always show up and we'll get you in. For class packs, we recommend booking 24 hours ahead to guarantee your spot.
What if I'm a beginner? +
All classes are coach-led with scaling options. Our coaches will adjust movements and intensity for your level on day one. Everyone starts somewhere. Start with a $50 trial week to get comfortable with our community.
