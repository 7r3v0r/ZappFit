Brand Notes
The Dark Luxury variant repositions Zapp Fitness as a premium, members-only training experience. The matte black foundation paired with warm gold (#C9A84C) accents creates an atmosphere of quiet exclusivity. Serif typography (Playfair Display for headlines, Cormorant Garamond for body) replaces the industrial sans-serifs, conveying sophistication over aggression. The warm white (#F5F2ED) and cream (#E8E2D6) tones soften the contrast against the deep blacks, evoking candlelit warmth suited to the basement studio environment. Every detail — thin gold borders, em-dash list markers, italic accents, generous letter-spacing — reinforces the premium, boutique positioning.
