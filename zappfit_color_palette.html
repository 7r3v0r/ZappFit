Theme notes: The Cyberpunk / Neon Noir palette replaces the original industrial green (#67DA32) accent with a tri-color neon system: cyan (#00F0FF) as the primary interactive color, magenta (#FF00FF) for section labels and decorative elements, and hot pink (#FF2D7B) for glitch effects. Backgrounds shift from pure black to deep blue-blacks with indigo undertones, evoking the look of a rain-slicked city at night. The glow variants are used extensively for text-shadow and box-shadow effects that simulate LED/neon lighting — designed to pop in the gym's low-light basement environment.
