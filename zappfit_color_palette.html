Theme notes: The Underground Fight Club theme replaces the original neon green (#67DA32) with a deep fight red (#D41F2E) as the primary accent. The palette is built on near-black backgrounds with warm-tinted grays, evoking raw concrete walls and cage-lit basement gyms. White text uses a warm off-white (#E8E4E0) instead of pure white for reduced glare in low-light environments. Red glow effects are used sparingly for depth, while dark blood red (#8B0000) provides structural borders. The overall feel is aggressive, gritty, and unapologetically intense.
